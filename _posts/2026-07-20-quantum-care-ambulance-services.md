**Project Description:** For our Data Analysis and Design module, my team and I were assigned to develop an information system for a business that would benefit the community in some way...

Many underprivileged communities in South Africa, especially those near remote mining towns, lack access to medical care due to distance and the cost of travel. Our team proposed that if patients cannot reach the clinic, 
then the clinic must be brought to them.

This idea gave rise to the **Quantum Care Ambulance Service (QCAS)**.  

**Project Date:** The Information System was created during the second semester of 2025, with planning and preparation beginning early in the first semester.

Our task was to provide QCAS with an information system capable of managing:
- Inventory for each ambulance
- The operational status of ambulances (available or dispatched)
- Service records for each vehicle
- Events attended by the ambulances

The system’s functionality was primarily designed and implemented using Visual Studio with C# code, while the database was built using SQL Management Studio.  

This solution was essential in providing the QCAS medical team and management staff with improved clarity and efficiency in their operations.

Through this project, I learned the importance of thoughtful design in ensuring efficiency and scalability in large systems.  

**Skills gained from this experience include:**
- Communication and collaboration management  
- Applying and strengthening my C# knowledge  
- Problem-solving using C#  
- Database development and design  
- SQL query language proficiency  
- GitHub version control implementation  
- Time management  

Overall, I am truly grateful to my team members. This level of success would not have been possible without their collaboration and dedication.
